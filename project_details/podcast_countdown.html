Podcast Countdown

A countdown app created using vanilla JavaScript to count down the days until the beginning of the 3rd season of The Green Horizon podcast. After the season began the countdown shifted to the days since season three began, and will shift again when the release date of the 4th season is announced. Simple clean design using colors from The Green Horizon artwork on their website, with links to listen to the podcast and the podcast’s social media.
